How it works
  1. Install the Nodisaar extension from the Chrome Web Store
  2. Click the Nodisaar icon in your toolbar to view 3 items from your friend's list
  3. Head to Netflix or Amazon Prime watch history to add your own favourites
  4. Share your list with friends — when one friend opens it, your friend's full list unlocks for you
